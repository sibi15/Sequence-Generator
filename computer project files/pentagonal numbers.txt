
> Pentagonal Numbers:
- A pentagonal number is a figurative number that extends the concept of triangular and square numbers to the pentagon.
- It is defined as the no. of dots that is obtained after overlaying a pentagon to the previous one, sharing the same vertex.

				.		1
			      .   .
			    .  . .  .		5
			    .   .   .
			      .   .             12 

- The general formula for the nth term is given by:
                 (3n^2 - n)/2
